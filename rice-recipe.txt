Ingredients:
 - 3 Cups water
 - Pinch of salt
 - 2 Cups of long grain white rice

 Directions:
 1. Bring 3 cups water to a boil with a pinch of salt.
 2. Stir in 2 cups of long grain white rice, cover with a tight fitting lid and turn the burner down to the lowest setting on your stove top. Set the timer for 20 minutes.
 3. When done, take off heat and let sit for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.
